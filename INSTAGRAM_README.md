## ‚ö° Key Principles

1. **Consistency > Perfection**
   - Post regularly, even if not perfect
   - Algorithm favors consistency
   - Your best post ever is worth nothing if you don't post again

2. **Educational > Salesy**
   - Teach something useful in every post
   - Let the teaching do the selling
   - "Hey, hire me" never works on Instagram

3. **Show Process > Show Results**
   - People connect with process
   - Showing results looks like bragging
   - Showing how you solve problems builds trust

4. **Engagement > Reach**
   - Respond to every comment
   - Ask questions in captions
   - Build a community, not an audience

5. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - 3 excellent posts > 7 mediocre posts
   - Focus on depth, not speed
   - Your audience wants to learn, not be entertained
